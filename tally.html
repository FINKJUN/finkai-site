FAQ

Questions about FINKAI and Tally.

How do I get automated MIS reports from Tally without manual work?
FINKAI connects to TallyPrime and Tally ERP 9 via Tally's XML gateway and replaces manual MIS report generation entirely. Finance teams ask questions in plain English — "What is the P&L for this month?", "Show me debtor ageing over 60 days", "What is the Trial Balance as of 31 March?" — and FINKAI computes answers from live Tally data in seconds. No exports to Excel, no waiting.
Does FINKAI work with TallyPrime 7?
Yes. FINKAI supports TallyPrime 4.x, 5.x, 6.x, and 7.x as well as Tally ERP 9 Release 6+. It connects via Tally's native XML gateway — both on-premise Tally installations and Tally on Cloud with remote access enabled. Multi-company configurations are fully supported.
Does FINKAI write anything back to Tally?
No. FINKAI is read-only. It connects to Tally's XML gateway to read ledger, voucher, and stock data — but never writes any data back to Tally. Your Tally data and entries are completely untouched.
Can I get ageing reports from multiple Tally companies at once?
Yes. FINKAI supports multi-company Tally setups and can query ageing, Trial Balance, P&L, and stock data across all your Tally companies simultaneously — aggregating results into a single consolidated view.
