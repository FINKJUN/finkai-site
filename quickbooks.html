FAQ

Questions about FINKAI and QuickBooks.

Can FINKAI give me automated financial reports from QuickBooks Online?
Yes. FINKAI connects to QuickBooks Online via OAuth 2.0 and answers financial questions in plain English — P&L, Balance Sheet, Trial Balance, A/R ageing, A/P ageing — computed from live QBO data in seconds. No exports, no Excel, no manual report preparation.
Does FINKAI support multi-company QuickBooks reporting?
Yes. FINKAI supports multi-company QuickBooks reporting via QuickBooks Online Accountant (QBOA). Finance teams can query financials across multiple QBO firms simultaneously — getting consolidated answers from all connected companies in a single interface.
Does FINKAI write any data to QuickBooks?
No. FINKAI is completely read-only. It connects to QuickBooks Online via OAuth 2.0 to read financial data but never writes any data back to QuickBooks. Your QuickBooks data and entries are completely untouched.
Which QuickBooks plans does FINKAI work with?
FINKAI works with all QuickBooks Online plans — Simple Start, Essentials, Plus, and Advanced — as well as QuickBooks Online Accountant (QBOA) for multi-firm access.
