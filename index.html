Why Determinism Matters

Finance demands numbers you can audit — not numbers an AI invented.

Determinism is the architectural choice that makes FINKAI trustworthy for board packs, statutory filings, and audit-level reporting.

⚙️

What is determinism in AI?

In AI, determinism means the system produces consistent, reproducible outputs from the same inputs — no randomness, no invented numbers.

  • Given identical ERP data, FINKAI always returns the same figure
  • No token sampling, no probabilistic generation
  • Every computation is arithmetic — not prediction
⚠️

Why it matters in finance

Financial decisions depend on accurate balance sheets, income statements, and variance analysis. A general-purpose AI in finance is not just a risk — it is a liability.

  • One wrong figure in a board pack and credibility is gone — permanently
  • Numbers must be auditable and traceable to source journal entries
  • SEBI and RBI filings require verifiable, reproducible computations
🔗

How FINKAI achieves it

FINKAI uses AI to understand your question, then purpose-built computation tools to produce the answer from your live ERP data — never from model memory or prediction.

  • Reads Trial Balance and GL directly from your ERP — never guesses
  • Every figure computed arithmetically and traceable to source GL codes
  • Self-hosted deployment means your data never reaches external servers

Security

Security Overview

How FINKAI protects your financial data.

Read-only ERP access
FINKAI connects to your ERP systems in read-only mode. It cannot write, modify, or delete any data in your source systems. Credentials are stored encrypted and never exposed to end users.

Self-hosted deployment
FINKAI can be deployed entirely within your own infrastructure — on-premise or private cloud. In self-hosted mode, your financial data never leaves your network and no external API calls are made to FINKAI servers.

Role-based access control
Access is enforced at the query engine level — not just the UI. Permissions are configurable at entity level, GL level, and report level, per user. SSO is supported.

Audit trail
Every query, every user action, and every data access event is logged with a full audit trail including user identity, timestamp, and query detail. Audit logs are immutable and exportable.

Data in transit
All data in transit is encrypted using TLS 1.2 or higher. ERP connections use encrypted channels. No financial data is transmitted over unencrypted connections.

Infrastructure
Cloud-hosted deployments run on AWS infrastructure. Data is stored within the region specified at onboarding. FINKAI does not store your financial data beyond what is required for query processing.
