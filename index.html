Can FINKAI replace SAP Crystal Reports for financial reporting?
Yes. FINKAI connects to SAP S/4HANA via standard OData APIs and reads the Universal Journal (ACDOCA) — enabling profit centre P&L, segment reporting, group consolidation, Trial Balance, and variance analysis in plain English. Building an Oracle OTBI analysis requires selecting subject areas, adding columns, setting filters, configuring prompts, formatting, and waiting for IT to grant access — often taking days. Both read from Oracle GL — FINKAI makes it accessible to any finance professional, not just OTBI-trained users. Works with Oracle Fusion Cloud and Oracle EBS R12.
