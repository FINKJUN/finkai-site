FAQ

Questions about FINKAI and SAP.

How do I get SAP financial reports without Crystal Reports or ABAP?
FINKAI connects to SAP S/4HANA via standard OData APIs and reads the Universal Journal (ACDOCA). CFOs and Controllers type financial questions in plain English and receive computed answers in seconds — profit centre P&L, consolidated Trial Balance, variance analysis — without any Crystal Reports, T-codes, or ABAP development required.
Can FINKAI replace FBL3N and FAGLB03 for financial reporting?
Yes. FINKAI reads the same data that T-codes like FBL3N, FBL5N, and FAGLB03 surface — but through a plain English interface. CFOs type a question and get the answer in seconds, without navigating SAP GUI or remembering T-code sequences.
Does FINKAI work with SAP ECC 6.0?
Yes. FINKAI supports SAP S/4HANA Cloud, SAP S/4HANA On-Premise 2020–2024, and SAP ECC 6.0 EHP6+. No ABAP development is required for any version.
What SAP financial dimensions can FINKAI query?
FINKAI reads all key SAP financial dimensions from the Universal Journal: company code, profit centre, segment, fiscal period, currency type, ledger (leading and extension), and business area. SAP Group Reporting dimensions including consolidation unit are also supported.
